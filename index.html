Who We Are

Google Developer Groups on Campus (GDG on Campus) at Dronacharya College of Engineering is a student-led developer community backed by Google Developers...

"We are looking for campus friends who are willing to share their expertise on Android, Kotlin, Firebase, Flutter, GenAI, Gemini, TensorFlow, or Google Cloud. Whether it's your own project or you want to give a tech talk, as long as you have a passion for technology, don't hesitate! We need simple, friendly, and fun student-led talks."
